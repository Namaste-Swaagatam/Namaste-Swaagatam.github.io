About Graha Pravesh

Graha Pravesh is a sacred Hindu ceremony performed when moving into a new home. It involves prayers for prosperity, peace, and positive energy in the new dwelling.

Significance

  • Purification of the new dwelling
  • Inviting positive energies
  • Seeking blessings for prosperity
  • Protection from negative influences
